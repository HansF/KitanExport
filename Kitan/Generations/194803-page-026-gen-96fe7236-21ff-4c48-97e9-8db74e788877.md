**The Pot of Do-Re-Mi**
**By Toshiyasu Hiratatsu**
**Illustrated by Minami NiyÅ**

Enter a little north from Shijo Kawaramachi, turn east, and in that alley, one modest bar stands alone. Of course, receiving the influence of the 7.1 Prohibition [Ordinance], on the front, a "closed for business" sign is hung, but anyway, because it is a bar-style house with a gaudy flower name, anyone who thinks to look it up will know immediately.
