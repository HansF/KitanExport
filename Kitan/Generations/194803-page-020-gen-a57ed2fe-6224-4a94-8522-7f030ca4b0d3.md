Though the shop was tucked away in an alley behind a cinema, it was surprisingly visible to the public. Because the proprietor was an honest man who never cheated on the weight of his goods, the shop was constantly thronged with customers. About six months later, a middle-aged woman moved into the second floor of a house on the corner of that same street.
