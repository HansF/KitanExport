## Literal English Translation

It is so. The parent is also the child of a person; therefore, being in this rank, it is necessary to have compassion and to pay attention, and this text seems to be the law/norm for nurturing people, truthfully.

Although people frequently speak of the Heavenly Way (Tendō) by mouth, the person who knows what kind of thing it is is rare, and it is merely kept secret as the 'reverence of the sun and moon' (Gachijitsu wo matsu yatsu'u no to), [but] the path by which people live is the Heavenly Way. To bestow Benevolence, Righteousness, and Wisdom—when one first performs the path of being a person, it manifests in their nature/disposition (saga). If it is based on the dense/important part of the human path, then it is the path of being a person. If it is located exactly outside (the self), it is the Heavenly Way of service. The Heavenly Way of serving parents. Within our own selves, it is the Heavenly Way of the sovereign lord (kunshu). The person who serves oneself is the Heavenly Way of the husband and wife. The Heavenly Way of yielding to others. Our own

[self/house] is vast, and consequently, if one listens well and reveres the Kami (gods), one fears both Heaven and the Path of Sincerity, and if one acts as if intending to break [it], then it is called the near Heavenly Way. Thus, if the human path accords [with this], then in addition to the word of truth (shingon),
if one swears an oath to the Kami, one protects the heart of deviation within this [path] even when praying, and this sincerity alone exists; if the Path of Sincerity is fulfilled, one will revere the Kami even without praying, and furthermore,
will not the spirit (mitama) of the lord be within the heart without evil intention? Do you not see the clarity (sakiki)? Does the moon not dwell in the turbulent river?
Or, it is said that the Heavenly Way is fulfilled in the place where the island's spouse (wife) spends/passes their sincerity. [This demonstrates that] even if one performs the ritual of worshiping Heaven by turning against a knowing heart, if one neglects the True Path, the path that lies outside of the heart shall certainly not be called correct.

## Literary English Translation

Such is the case. The parent, too, is merely the child of another person; thus, holding this station, one must act with compassion and vigilance. This text ought truthfully to be treated as the essential doctrine for the care of humanity.

Though many speak endlessly of the Heavenly Way (Tendō), rare is the person who genuinely understands its nature. It is often kept obscure, equated merely with the ritual of venerating the sun and moon. Yet, the path by which humankind lives is itself the Heavenly Way. To embody Benevolence, Righteousness, and Wisdom—this is the initial execution of the human path, manifesting in one's intrinsic nature. If this path is deeply rooted in the substance of human life, it is the Path of the Person. When one is situated outside this immediate sphere, it becomes the Heavenly Way of service; the Heavenly Way of filial piety toward parents. Within our own domain, it is the Heavenly Way of the sovereign lord; the person who serves oneself is the Heavenly Way of the husband and wife; the Heavenly Way of deferring to others. Our own [being]

is extensive, and consequently, if one listens attentively and reveres the gods, one must fear both Heaven and the Path of Sincerity. If one acts as if intending to shatter this Way, that is what is called the immediate Heavenly Way. Therefore, if one’s conduct aligns with the Path of the Person, then beyond mere truthful declaration,
if one pledges an oath to the Kami, even one's prayers will protect the heart from internal deviation. If this sincerity alone resides, and the Path of Sincerity is fully realized, one will naturally revere the Kami even without formal prayer. Furthermore,
will not the spirit of the ruler dwell purely within the heart, free of all wickedness? Do you not perceive the perfect clarity? Does the moon not perfectly lodge its reflection in the muddy stream?
Alternatively, it is said that the Heavenly Way is fulfilled even in the remote place where a wife on an island maintains her sincerity. This proves that even if one turns against a heart that knows better and performs the rite of worshipping Heaven, if the True Path is neglected, the path existing outside the heart cannot rightly be called correct.
