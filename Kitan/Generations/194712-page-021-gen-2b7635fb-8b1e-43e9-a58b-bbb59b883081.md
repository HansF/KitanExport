*   **痴態 (Chitai):** Lewd or disgraceful/shameless behavior. A strong, morally judgmental term used for sexual activities considered taboo or vulgar for respectable society.
*   **嘗て (Katsute):** Old kana usage (歴史的仮名遣い) for かつて (once, formerly).
*   **交はす (Kawasuru):** Old kana usage for 交わす (to exchange), meaning "to have sexual intercourse" in this context (euphemistic).
*   **沈鬱 (Chin'utsu):** Deep melancholy, deep depression or gloom. Used to pathologize the wife's state, suggesting psychological need rather than simple lust.
*   **倒錯 (Tōsaku):** Perversion, psychological inversion. A key word in post-war discussions of sexual psychology.
*   **責め苦 (Semeku):** Torment, abuse, torture. In the context of S/M (SM的), it refers specifically to the dominant partner's acts of discipline or pain infliction.
*   **逢瀬 (Ōse):** Secret, usually illicit, romantic or sexual rendezvous.
*   **堕落の極致 (Daraku no Kyokuchi):** The absolute extreme of degradation/corruption. Highly dramatic language used to emphasize the sensational nature of the climax.
