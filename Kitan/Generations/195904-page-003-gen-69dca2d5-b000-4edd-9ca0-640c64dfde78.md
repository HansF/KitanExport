*   **Honorable Mention:** 3,000 Yen (several winners)

**Submission Guidelines:**
*   **Genres:** Fiction, creative writing, confessions, memoirs, personal accounts, and other subjects suitable for this magazine.
*   **Length:** Approximately 30 to 50 pages (standard 400-character manuscript paper). Slight variations in length are acceptable.
*   **Deadline:** No fixed deadline for the time being. Submissions will be reviewed in the order received, and winning entries will be published starting with the latest issues.
*   **Note:** To distinguish from regular reader submissions, please write "Prize Manuscript" on the first page of your entry.
*   **Announcements:** Winners will be notified via the dispatch of their prize money. The publication of the work in the magazine will serve as the formal announcement.
*   **Format:** Please use 200 or 400-character manuscript paper and send via 5th-class mail (8 yen per 100g).

**General Reader Submissions:**
We also welcome regular submissions in the following categories: Original Fiction, Memoirs, Media Reports (Film/Magazines), "My Images" (creative concepts), Ideas for the Magazine, and Newspaper/Weekly Report Clippings. **Subscription Information:**
*Kitan Kurabu* is available exclusively through direct mail and is not sold in bookstores.
