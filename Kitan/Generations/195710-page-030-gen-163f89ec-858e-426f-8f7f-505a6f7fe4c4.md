This texture is difficult to express in Japanese script; Roman letters are far more appropriate. I have always felt that Hiragana is too abstract and lacks reality for these sounds. I propose that the authors of magazines like *Kiki* [*Kitan Club*] take the initiative to research "Screams" properly—perhaps by tormenting models while a tape recorder runs—to find the perfect Romanization. I imagine that in itself would make for a fine pictorial feature on torture...

Furthermore, the wailing of mature women is rarely witnessed, though it can be discovered in special circumstances. It is said that the more beautiful the woman, the more animalistic her screaming becomes—something like *"UWA / UWA!"*—intense and wild. The loud voice of a mature woman crying in pain is close to the roar of a beast, hardly different from that of a man, which is perhaps why it garners so little interest. There are almost no novels to be found that depict this realistically.

There is a story I once heard from a nurse, however...
