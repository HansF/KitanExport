**Night of the Banquet**

(1) It is a night banquet of magic-users held at midnight on Saturdays with the Demon King as the organizer. The places where it is held are inside forests, wastelands, caverns, abandoned castles, graveyards, etc.
(2) As for the Great Sabbath, every three months, finally when the night the banquet is held comes, the members of the magic-users straddle things like brooms and fly through the deep-sinking night sky like meteors.
